About Us

Borahae Café is a casual cafe that serves Western food located in Machang, Kelantan. It was founded by 3 founders, Nur Aznie binti Aziz, Nur Hafizah Izzati binti Ahmad Sabrie and Sarah Sajidah binti Saiful Sany in 2022.

“Borahae” or “I Purple You” means “I’ll love you till the end of days” since purple or violet is the last color of the rainbow. The phrase combines two Korean words: violet (bora) and I love you (saranghae). In other words, this café is suitable for Army or BTS fandom to hang out or holding an event.
Borahae Café serves from main dishes to desserts and beverages. We stick to the principle that we serve everyone at affordable prices.

Additionally, we offer catering and takeout services for any event, from small parties to corporate gatherings.

Both tiny families and significant international enterprises are among our clientele.
